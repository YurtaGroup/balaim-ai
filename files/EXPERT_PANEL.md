## Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Application
Expert submits:
- Full name, specialization
- Medical license number
- Years of experience
- Current clinic / practice
- Why they want to join balam.ai
- 1 photo (professional but warm)

### Step 2: Verification (balam.ai team)
- Verify license with KG Ministry of Health registry
- Background check — no disciplinary actions
- Confirm active practice
- Reference call (optional for Phase 1)

### Step 3: Partnership Call with Founder
- Explain mission and community values
- Agree: minimum 5 community responses/week
- Set consultation pricing (expert decides, balam.ai takes 30%)
- Sign partnership agreement
- Timeline: profile live within 7 days of signing

### Step 4: Profile Setup (balam.ai helps)
- Professional photo (balam.ai arranges Bishkek photographer)
- Bio writing (balam.ai copywriter assists — warm, not clinical)
- First 3 articles written together
- Expert reviews 20 sample community posts to calibrate tone

### Step 5: Shadow Period (2 weeks)
- Expert observes community feed, no paid consults yet
- Responds to 2–3 posts/day to build presence
- balam.ai community manager monitors and gives feedback
- After 2 weeks: full activation, paid consultations enabled

## Community Participation Standards
- Respond to all posts tagged "needs doctor" within 24 hours
- Minimum 5 quality community responses/week
- Every response includes: explanation + practical advice + when to see a doctor in person
- Never diagnose definitively in community posts (liability + medical ethics)
- Can and should correct dangerous misinformation from other users
- Can "adopt" post as educational resource (anonymized with mom permission)

---

## Consultation Pricing Guide (starting ranges)
- Video call (30 min): 800–1,500 KGS
- Text consultation (24h written response): 400–800 KGS
- Follow-up (same issue, same week): 50% discount
- Emergency same-day slot: 200% regular price

balam.ai takes 30% of all paid consultations.
Minimum guaranteed earnings during first 3 months: 10,000 KGS/month
(balam.ai tops up if consultations don't reach this threshold)

---

## Quality Standards
- Response helpfulness rating: target >4.7/5
- Response timeliness: 24h for community, 4h for paid consults (pediatrician)
- Monthly review call with founder
- If rating drops below 4.3 for 2 consecutive weeks: corrective conversation
- Removal criteria: license issue, repeated low ratings, no-shows on paid consults

---

## What Experts Get From balam.ai
1. Brand building — "balam.ai's verified pediatrician" is a strong local credential
2. New patient pipeline — community users convert to clinic patients
3. Supplemental income — consultation fees, no overhead
4. Content platform — articles under their name, permanent searchable
5. Insight — see what moms are actually asking in real-time (anonymized)
6. Mission — genuinely help more mothers than a clinic schedule allows
