## Talking points for the call

1. **Frame (2 min):** "Мам, I've been building Balam into a real family health platform. After what happened with Uncle, I'm doubling down — we're going to put a home diagnostic kit in 10,000 families in the next 18 months so nobody else dies because they never got their sugar checked. I need you on this, not just as a consulting endocrinologist, but as the clinical lead for the whole company."
2. **What the role is (3 min):** Medical Director. You set the clinical rules of the road — what the AI is allowed to say, what must escalate to a human, which thresholds flag a red alert. ~5 hours/week for the next 6 months, dropping to ~3 once we hire a second MD.
3. **Why it matters for her (2 min):** This is her name on the science. Every preeclampsia caught, every diabetes picked up early — that's her clinical footprint scaled by software. She's 55, became an NP late, and this is the thing that outlasts any single clinic.
4. **The ask (1 min):** Sign the attached LOI. 1% founding clinical advisor equity (on standard 4-year vest, 1-year cliff), $1,500/month stipend starting when we close seed. Named CMO in the Series-A deck.
5. **Reassurance:** "Mom, we're going to write down scope-of-practice clearly so you're never asked to sign off on anything you haven't actually seen. This isn't Theranos — every flagged case goes to a human, you're not rubber-stamping AI."
