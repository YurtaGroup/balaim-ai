## Description (4000 char max)

Balam.AI is your AI-powered parenting companion, from pregnancy planning through your baby's first years.

Named after the Mayan word for jaguar — a protector — Balam is here to empower you on every step of your parenting journey.

**Pregnancy Tracker**
Week-by-week development, baby size, symptoms, and personalized insights. Track weight, water, sleep, and kicks. Know exactly what's happening with your baby at every stage.

**Balam AI Companion**
Ask anything. Get warm, personalized answers grounded in your current week and tracking data. "Is this cramp normal at 24 weeks?" gets a real answer, not a generic one.

**Baby Development (0-24 months)**
Evidence-based milestones, activities, feeding guides, sleep patterns, vaccine schedules, and red flags — curated month by month. Know when to celebrate and when to call the doctor.

**Community**
Stage-matched groups with other parents at exactly your week. Q&A with verified doctors and midwives. Never feel alone.

**Professional Network**
Find OB-GYNs, pediatricians, midwives, lactation consultants, and doulas in your area. Book consultations directly.

**Marketplace**
Curated baby products recommended for your stage — from pregnancy pillows at week 20 to size M diapers at month 6. No more overwhelming Amazon rabbit holes.

**For Moms AND Dads**
Partners have their own spaces and get age-appropriate tips for being involved at every stage.

Privacy-first. Your data is yours. No ads. No selling your info.

Download Balam.AI and never wonder "is this normal?" again.
